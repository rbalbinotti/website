1. Smart Supply Chain AI: Predictive Demand Engine & Stochastic Data Generation

Integration of high-performance time-series predictive modeling and columnar data engineering applied to the supply chain.

  • Stack:
  • Objective: Mitigate catastrophic inventory shortages (stockouts) and optimize stock turnover (Inventory Turnover Rate) across complex supply chains.
  • Engineering Approach: Replaced slow statistical approaches with modern, ultra-fast processing pipelines using Polars, handling massive volumes in columnar format (Parquet). The predictive modeling was built on advanced gradient boosting frameworks (LightGBM + MLForecast), incorporating high-frequency macroeconomic and weather exogenous variables (via INMET data).
  • Public Validation & Digital Twin: To validate and test the scalability of the ecosystem without compromising proprietary data, I pioneered and published a stochastic engine that generates complex synthetic data. The dataset was adopted by the community and is publicly available for Big Data testing.
  • Outcome: Theoretical reduction in storage costs and immediate optimization of the raw materials logistics workflow.

2. Personalized Medicine: Deep Learning Diagnostics with Ethical Rigor

Advanced pathology classification in medical imaging focusing on algorithm interpretability and explainability (XAI).

  • Stack:
  • Objective: Develop clinical decision support systems with high diagnostic accuracy and full regulatory transparency for hospital or integrated healthcare contexts.
  • Engineering Approach: Implemented Convolutional Neural Network architectures (custom CNNs) optimized via hardware acceleration (NVIDIA CUDA). The key differentiator of this project lies in its governance and ethics layer: utilizing AI Explainability (SHAP and LIME) techniques to map precisely which regions of the exams or clinical attributes influenced the AI's decision-making. This eliminates the "black box" issue, which is a crucial requirement for regulatory validations and international audits (such as European AI regulations).
  • Outcome: Predictive modeling with a validated accuracy exceeding 90% in severe scenarios and complete logical auditing of all decision-making steps.
